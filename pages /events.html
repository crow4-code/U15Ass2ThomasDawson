About Us

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester, we believe that Slovak culture should be experienced, not just learned. That’s why we organise a variety of events for everyone—families, children, adults, and individuals who simply want to connect with Slovak heritage, make new friends, and enjoy great company.

Easter Workshop (6th April):

Step into the world of Slovak Easter traditions at our Easter Workshop, where culture, creativity, and community come together for a fun and welcoming afternoon. This hands-on event will offer something for everyone—families, individuals, and anyone who enjoys arts, crafts, and festive traditions. Join us in decorating eggs using traditional Slovak techniques, creating beautiful Easter crafts, and learning about the unique customs that make Slovak Easter so special.
